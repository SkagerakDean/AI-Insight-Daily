### Tracking Technologies and Cookies
We're using Cookies and other similar tracking tech to keep an eye on what's happening on our Service and to save a few tidbits of info. These tracking tools include beacons, tags, and scripts, all designed to gather and track data, helping us spruce up and analyze our Service. What kind of tech are we talking about? Well, it might include:

- **Cookies or Browser Cookies**: Think of a Cookie as a tiny file that lands on your Device. You can totally tell your browser to reject all Cookies or to give you a heads-up when one's being sent. Just a heads-up though, if you don't accept Cookies, some parts of our Service might not play nice. But hey, unless you've tweaked your browser settings to give Cookies the cold shoulder, our Service will likely be using them.
- **Web Beacons**: Some parts of our Service and our emails might have these tiny digital files called web beacons (aka clear GIFs, pixel tags, or single-pixel GIFs). They're basically little trackers that let us count users who've visited a page or opened an email. Super handy for website stats, like seeing what's popular or making sure our systems are running smoothly!

Cookies come in two flavors: "Persistent" or "Session" Cookies. Persistent ones stick around on Your personal computer or mobile device even after you go offline. Session Cookies? They vanish the moment you close your web browser. Poof!

So, why do we use both Session and Persistent Cookies? For these reasons:

- **Necessary / Essential Cookies**
  - **Type**: Session Cookies
  - **Managed by**: Us
  - **Purpose**: These are the rockstar Cookies! They're absolutely crucial for getting you the services available on the Website and letting you use some of its awesome features. They also help us verify users and keep those pesky fraudulent account uses at bay. Seriously, without these, the services you're asking for just wouldn't happen, and we only use them to make sure you get 'em.

- **Cookies Policy / Notice Acceptance Cookies**
  - **Type**: Persistent Cookies
  - **Managed by**: Us
  - **Purpose**: These Cookies are like a little flag that tells us if you've already given us the thumbs-up to use cookies on the Website. Simple!

- **Functionality Cookies**
  - **Type**: Persistent Cookies
  - **Managed by**: Us
  - **Purpose**: These clever Cookies let us remember your choices when you're cruising around the Website, like your login info or what language you prefer. The whole point? To give you a more personalized experience, so you don't have to re-enter your preferences every single time you visit. Sweet!

Want the lowdown on the Cookies we use and what choices you have? Just swing by our Cookies Policy or the Cookies section right here in our Privacy Policy.
